Terms & Conditions

Welcome to ReNew! We help you give your old furniture a second chance. Our team restores your pieces with care and creativity, bringing them back to life. By using our service, you agree to the following terms:

Pickup

We offer scheduled pickups based on availability in your area. Please make sure the furniture is ready and accessible on the agreed day. If the item is too damaged, unsafe to handle, or doesn’t fit our restoration criteria, we may not be able to accept it—but we’ll always explain why.

Restoration

Each piece is unique, and we restore it using the best methods while keeping its original style. Got special requests? Just tell us—some may cost extra, and we’ll confirm before we start.

Payment

Our pricing depends on the condition, size, and complexity of the work. A deposit might be required before we begin, and the remaining balance is due when we deliver your restored piece. We’ll always give you a clear quote upfront—no surprises.

Responsibility

We handle all furniture with care, but we’re not responsible for any hidden damage or structural issues that existed before restoration. That said, we’ll always keep you informed during the process if we find anything unexpected.
